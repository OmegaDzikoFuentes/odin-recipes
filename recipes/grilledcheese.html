Grilled Cheese Sandwhich

Fresh bread toasted with gooey cheese

This is a classic simple snack and a great soup paring.

Ingridients

  • bread
  • 1 slice sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 slice colby jack
  • butter

Steps

  1. butter outside slices of bread
  2. place both slices of cheese inside bread
  3. sautee over meadium high heat skillet for 93 seconds (or until golden brown) on each side
